Covid-19: An Interactive Story

Social Distance. Lockdown. Flatten the Curve.
You've heard it all, but what does it really mean?
Why should you care?
This DIY story explains what these things are and how they are supposed to help us combat the Coronavirus.

This story will use simple simulations of a Coronavirus outbreak in the real world.
You will be able to interact with these simulations to try out the effects of different measures for yourself.

A box with bouncing balls will represent a group of people, such as a city.
To start with, some people will be infected with Coronavirus.
The virus can spread throughout the community when people come in contact with eachother.
